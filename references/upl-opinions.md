# Arizona UPL (Unauthorized Practice of Law) Advisory Opinions

**What this is:** A **separate** State Bar of Arizona advisory-opinion series addressing the **unauthorized practice of law (UPL)** — distinct from the Formal Ethics Opinions indexed in `opinions-index.md`. UPL opinions address what constitutes the practice of law and who may engage in it (nonlawyers, out-of-state lawyers, document preparers, ABS/LP scope, etc.).

**Authority context:** UPL in Arizona is governed by **Ariz. R. Sup. Ct. 31** (and Rule 31.1 on who is authorized to practice, including LPs and ABS entities under the 2021 reforms) and **ER 5.5** (Unauthorized Practice of Law; Multijurisdictional Practice). The advisory UPL opinions interpret these authorities; like ethics opinions, they are **advisory and non-binding.**
